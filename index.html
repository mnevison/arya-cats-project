SMALL CATS

  1. There are over 100 different cat breeds
  2. The average age for an indoor cat is 15 years while the average age for an outdoor cat is only 3-5 years
  3. The largest breed of cat is the Ragdoll. Males weigh 12-20 pounds, with females weighing 10-15 pounds
  4. Cats can see in colour – they are partially colour blind
  5. Cats are the sleepiest of all mammals. They spend an average of 16 hours sleeping each day
  6. A cat can jump 5 times as high as it is tall
  7. The oldest cat known, lived to the grand age of 36
  8. Cats respond more readily to names that end in an “ee” sound
  9. The nose pad of a cat is ridged in a pattern that is unique, just like the fingerprint of a human
  10. Cats lap liquid from the underside of their tongue rather than from the top when drinking
